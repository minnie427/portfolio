WHAT IS IT?

An immersive stage experience using a microphone as an input and colours as main focus


Primary Software: TouchDesigner

DESIGN APPROACH

The purpose of this project is to enhance the experience of the joy of singing, creating a fun and engaging experience, and to encourage users to enjoy and embrace their own voices. Users can enjoy the music and the colors that are generated according to their own voice, creating an immersive digital experience of sound and colors.


This project provides an immersive stage experience where colors on the screen react to sound (using voice input through microphones) and the body movement (using hand tracking) to change the colors on the screen and the sound output accordingly.


While the song is being played, the colors according to the musical notes will accumulate and blend with each other on the screen. At the end of the song, users will be able to see the result of all the colors generated by their voice, resulting in the color of their voice.
